🇬🇧 The **Computer Science Club** is pleased to invite 11th and 12th grade students to take part in the **Computer Science Rally Paper**, organized at the Sciences and Technology Campus (CST) of Saint Joseph University of Beirut in Mar Roukos.

**Details:**

📅 **Date:** Saturday, May 31, 2025

🕙 **Time:** 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM

📍 **Location:** Sciences and Technology Campus (CST), Mar Roukos

👥 **Target audience:** Teams of 3 students - open to 11th and 12th grade students

**Program highlights:**

🔍 Logic puzzles

💻 Programming challenges

🎮 Interactive and fun activities

An **USJ scholarship** will be awarded to the winning team!

🚨 Please note that spots are limited, students should register while forming a group of 3 using the link below:

👉 Registration Form : [https://forms.office.com/r/KsyvWYx1pM](https://forms.office.com/r/KsyvWYx1pM)

For any further information or questions, feel free to contact the : [+961 81 385 080](tel:96181385080).
